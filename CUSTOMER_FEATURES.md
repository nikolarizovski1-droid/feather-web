# Feather — Customer features

Feather is a **restaurant marketing and operations platform**: one dashboard controls your digital menu, promotions, events, in-venue screens, and table service. The sections below summarize what matters most for venues and their guests.

---

## Instant digital menu (QR, no app install)

Guests open your menu from a QR code: on **iOS**, **App Clip** delivers an instant, no–App Store install flow; on **Android**, the menu opens in the **mobile web** experience—fast, smooth, and without a separate app install. There is no sign-up wall in the way of browsing and ordering.

---

## AI-powered menu setup

Upload a **photo or PDF** of your paper menu and Feather uses AI to build a structured digital menu, including **translation and categories**, in minutes. Most venues can go live in **under 10 minutes** without developers or technical setup.

---

## Product banners and featured items

**Hero banners** and top products are the first marketing touchpoint in the guest session—fully controlled from your admin. A single tap can **add to cart**, open an **event** for booking, or send guests to **any URL**, ideal for specials, high-margin items, or partner promotions.

---

## Auto push notifications

**Every QR scan** can schedule a **timed** promo notification (delay set by you, e.g. 10 minutes or longer) so guests see your offer while intent is still high. Promos support **image or video**, copy, and an **Order now** call-to-action, managed entirely from the admin—no extra campaigns to run manually.

---

## Event promotions and calendar reminders

Event promos work on the same idea: after a scan, guests get a notification at your chosen delay with a rich event screen. They can **Book now** (e.g. call the venue) or **Remind me** to **save the event to their calendar** in one tap, reducing flyers and follow-up calls.

---

## TV Display App

Turn venue screens into a **live slideshow** of products, events, and partner or supplier ads, with **per-slide timing** and updates pushed from the admin in **real time**. One content pipeline feeds both guest phones and in-venue displays.

---

## Call Waiter and Call Receipt

Guests use **one-tap** buttons to request a server or the bill; alerts go **straight to staff** with the **exact table number**. That cuts missed signals, speeds service, and supports better turnover and guest satisfaction.

---

## Admin dashboard

**One place** manages regions and tables, staff and permissions, categories and pricing, **QR codes and tracking**, banners, notifications, events, **TV scheduling**, and branding—with **real-time sync** across channels. No separate tools or duplicate logins for your core guest journey.

---

## Staff apps (iOS and Android)

Dedicated **staff apps** deliver **real-time** alerts for service requests, with table context, plus **menu update** notifications and **multi-venue** support where your plan allows. Front of house stays aligned without shouting across the room.

---

## Analytics

The **analytics dashboard** ties scans, views, orders, and service calls into **live KPIs**, funnels, traffic sources, peak hours, and product performance. You can see what converts—from banners and notifications to orders—and adjust with data instead of guesswork.

---

## Plans and trial (summary)

**Starter** covers a digital menu with QR (iOS App Clip and Android web), and one-tap service; **Growth** adds unlimited products, banners, auto notifications, events, and analytics; **Pro** adds **multi-location**, the **TV app**, staff apps, multi-region tools, and priority support. Paid plans include a **30-day trial** (card on file; **no charge until day 31**; cancel anytime before then).
