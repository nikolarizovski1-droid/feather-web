One-Tap Service Request

Just below your business info, guests will see two clear action buttons: Call Waiter and Call Receipt.    With a single tap, guests can request service at their table or ask for the bill. There's no need for waving, waiting, or wondering if someone noticed. Each request goes straight to your staff’s Admin Application with the table number, so the right server knows exactly where to go.   This simple feature reduces delays, improves table turnover, and ensures that every guest feels attended to.    Faster service keeps guests happy and when guests are happy, they order more and tip better. It’s a small detail that makes a big difference in both guest satisfaction and staff efficiency.


Banner

Right below the service buttons, guests will see a horizontal banner image. You set this up in your Admin application by picking a product and uploading an image.   It’s perfect for promoting featured items like signature cocktails or desserts, as well as events or external pages. When a guest taps the banner, a product is added to the cart list, an event page opens for booking, or the user is redirected to the linked page. No searching, no extra steps.   This small feature creates big value. It helps drive more sales of high-margin items, gives you full control over what you spotlight, and even opens the door to paid promotions from suppliers. It’s a simple way to increase revenue without disrupting the guest experience.


Top Product

This section puts your most important dishes front and centre. You control what appears here — whether it's high-margin items, seasonal specials, or products you need to sell quickly. It’s the first thing guests see when they open the menu, making it easier for them to decide and order.   With the “See All” button, guests can explore the full list if they want more options, but this section helps guide attention where it matters most. It’s a simple, effective way to boost sales, reduce waste, and highlight the items that drive your business forward.

Product Promotion

When a guest scans the menu QR code, the app automatically schedules a local promotion notification using the delay set by the restaurant, whether that's 10 minutes or longer. This happens on every scan, helping your venue stay top-of-mind while purchase intent is still high. If the app is opened another way, it checks whether the promo was already shown and schedules it if needed.   Tapping the notification opens a promo screen with a featured image or video, custom title, description, and an “Order now” button. All content is managed directly from the admin app.   The result is more repeat orders, stronger upsell performance, and additional space for paid supplier campaigns, without paper ads or manual follow-up.

Event Promotion

Just like product promotions, if an event notification is enabled, scanning the menu QR code triggers a local reminder at the delay configured by the restaurant, whether that's 10 minutes or longer. This keeps the event top-of-mind even after the guest leaves. If the app is opened another way, it checks whether the notification was delivered and schedules it if needed.

 Tapping the notification opens an event screen with a custom image or video, title, and description, all managed from the admin app. Guests then see two clear actions: “Book now” to call the venue directly, and “Remind me” to add the event to their calendar in one tap.

  This makes event promotion effortless, helps guests take action faster, and fills more seats without adding staff workload.
